Did you know?

Research by Adams et al. (2021, Nature) shows that people systematically overlook subtractive solutions — even when they are simpler and cheaper. The minimum valid solution to this task requires only 1 fold. At 1,000 units, choosing 3 folds over 1 costs an extra €1,700 per 1,000 units.
